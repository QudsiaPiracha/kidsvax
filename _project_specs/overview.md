# Project Overview

## Vision
KidsVax digitizes the German Gelbes Heft and Impfpass into a single, mobile-friendly web app. Parents add their child's date of birth and instantly get the full U-Untersuchungen schedule and STIKO vaccination timeline -- with email reminders, milestone tracking, growth charts, and a shareable digital Teilnahmekarte.

## Goals
- [ ] Replace the paper U-Heft and Impfpass with a unified digital experience
- [ ] Auto-calculate all U-exam and vaccination due dates from DOB
- [ ] Send timely email reminders so parents never miss a checkup or vaccination
- [ ] Provide Masernschutzgesetz compliance tracking for daycare/school entry
- [ ] Support bilingual usage (German + English) for expat parents
- [ ] Deliver a clean, warm, mobile-first UI that works in a pediatrician's waiting room

## Non-Goals
- Medical advice or diagnosis
- EHR/ePA integration (Phase 3+)
- Pediatrician-side portal (Phase 4)
- Insurance billing or claims
- Social features or community

## Success Metrics
- Parent adds a child and sees full schedule in < 60 seconds
- Zero missed exams/vaccinations when reminders are enabled
- LCP < 2.5s on mobile 3G
- Lighthouse > 90 across all categories
